Figure 3. Light squash of a larval polytene nucleus where the chromosomes are surrounded by EAST and Megator labeling. The images are from a triple labeling of EAST with pAb IgY (green), Megator with mAb 12F10 (red), and the DNA with Hoechst (blue). The composite image in (A) shows that EAST surrounds the chromosomes and the composite image in (D) shows that Megator and EAST largely co-localize as indicated by the predominantly yellow color.
