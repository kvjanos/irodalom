Supplementary information S1 (movie) | Autophagosome formation

The formation of an autophagosome is visualized by monitoring a green fluorescent protein (GFP)-ATG5 fusion protein in mouse embryonic stem cells through time-lapse video microscopy (5 minutes elapsed time). ATG5 localizes on the phagophore membrane during elongation and dissociates from the membrane upon completion of autophagosome formation. Movie provided by Noboru Mizushima (Tokyo Medical and Dental University).
