Tumor necrosis factor-related apoptosis-inducing ligand (TRAIL) is required for induction of autophagy during lumen formation in vitro

Supporting Methods

Protein extraction and Western blot analysis from MCF-10A acini. Acinar structures were washed briefly with 4ºC PBS with protease inhibitors (10 m g/ml PMSF/1 m g/ml leupeptin/1 m g/ml aprotinin/1 m g/ml pepstatin) and then treated for 15 min at 4ºC with RIPA lysis buffer (150 mM NaCl/20 mM Tris, pH 7.5/0.1% SDS/1.0% sodium deoxycholate/1.0% Triton X-100). Matrigel (BD Biosciences) and acini were collected into a 1.5-ml centrifuge tube and pulled through a 27-gauge needle three to five times before placing on ice for 15 min. Protein concentrations were determined by using the BCA Protein Assay (Pierce). We loaded 50 m l of lysate onto 12% polyacrylamide gels, transferred the gels to immunoblot poly(vinylidene difluoride) membrane (BioRad), and washed them four times with Tris-buffered saline (TBS)-Tween 20. Blots were then incubated with appropriate horseradish peroxidase-conjugated secondary Ab, washed four times, reacted with enhanced chemiluminescence (NEN), and subjected to autoradiography. For stripping, the blots were incubated in 0.2 M NaOH for 15 min and washed with distilled water.

Morphogenesis Assays. Eight-chamber RS glass slides (BD Biosciences) were coated with 30 m l of Matrigel per well and allowed to gel for 30 min at 37ºC. Cells were resuspended in assay medium (DMEM-F12/2% horse serum/0.5 m g/ml hydrocortisone/100 ng/ml cholera toxin/10 m g/ml insulin) containing 1% Matrigel and 5 ng/ml epidermal growth factor at a concentration of 1 × 104 cells per ml. We added 500 m l to each Matrigel-coated chamber. Assay medium containing 5 ng/ml epidermal growth factor and 2% Matrigel was replaced every 4 days.

Generation of MCF-10A Cell Lines. Truncated TRAIL-R1 and TRAIL-R2 cDNAs were generated by PCR using pCMV1-Flag-DR4 and pCMV1-Flag-DR5 (kindly provided by Avi Ashkenazi, Genentech) as templates and the following primers: 5'Flag-CCCGGATCCACCACCATGTCTGCACTTCTGATC, 3'DR4-CCCGAATTCTCACAAGCGCCAGAAACA, and 3'DR5-CCCGAATTCTCAACCACCTGAGCAGAT. PCR fragments containing Flag-tagged DR4(amino acids 24–283) and Flag-tagged DR5 (amino acids 52–222) were cloned into EcoR1/BamH1 sites of pBabepuro. Vesicular stomatitis virus (VSV)-pseudotyped retroviruses were produced by transfection of the VSV-GPG producer cell line (1) with 15 m g of DNA by using Lipofectamine (Invitrogen). Retrovirus-containing supernatants were collected at days 5–7 after transfection and were stored at –80ºC. MCF-10A cells were plated at 4 × 105 cells per 10-cm dish and infected with the retroviruses mentioned above. Stable populations were obtained by selection with the appropriate drug ([2 m g/ml puromycin (Sigma) or 200 m g/ml hygromycin (Calbiochem)]. Stable pools of MCF-10A cells that coexpressed Bcl-XL and FADD-DN or TruncR1/2 were generated by infecting with Bcl-XL and then selecting cells with hygromycin, followed by infection with FADD-DN or TruncR1/2 virus and selection with puromycin.

Immunofluorescence (IF) Analysis and Image Acquisition. IF analysis of MCF-10A acini was performed as described (2). Briefly, structures were fixed in 2% formalin (Sigma), permeabilized in 0.3% Triton X-100 in PBS for 10 min at 4°C. Fixed structures were washed three times in PBS/glycine (130 mM NaCl/7 mM Na2HPO4/3.5 mM NaH2PO4/100 mM glycine) for 10 min each. The washed structures were blocked first in IF buffer (130 mM NaCl/7 mM Na2HPO4/3.5 mM NaH2PO4/7.7 mM NaN3/0.1% BSA/0.2% Triton X-100/0.05% Tween 20) plus 10% goat serum (GS) for 1–2 h and subsequently with "secondary blocking buffer" (IF buffer/10% GS/20 m g ml–1 of goat anti-mouse F(ab')2) for 60 min. Structures were incubated overnight in primary Ab at 4ºC after which unbound Ab was removed with IF buffer. Secondary Abs coupled with Alexa Fluor dyes (Molecular Probes) were diluted in IF buffer containing 10% GS and incubated for 60 min. Unbound secondary Abs were washed as described above. Finally, the structures were incubated with PBS containing 0.5 ng/ml DAPI (Roche) and 1 m M TO-PRO-3 (Molecular Probes) for 15 min before mounting with Prolong anti-fade reagent (Molecular Probes).

1. Ory, D. S., Neu geboren, B.-A & Mulligan, R. C. (1996) Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. USA 93, 11400–11406.

2. Anglade, P., Vyas, S., Javoy-Agid, F., Herrero, M. T., Michel, P. P., Marquez, J., Mouatt-Prigent, A., Ruberg, M., Hirsch, E. C. & Agid, Y. (1997) Histol. Histopathol. 12, 25–31.
